Automatic Assigning Tasks

for team members

Sprint planning

Family chores

Joint project of students

Tasks that should be done by a team must been well planned.

When you assigned your tasks you must threat these parameters:
  • The priority of the tasks.
  • The amount of the tasks.
  • The allotted time of the tasks.
  • The availability of the team members.
  • The expertise of the team members.
Assigning tasks manually each period is a complex task since all the parameters above are dynamically changed.
